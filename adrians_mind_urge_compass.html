10-minute urge reset

1
Name it“Boredom is here.” “The urge to trade is here.” Do not turn it into your identity.
2
Find it in the bodyNotice pressure, heat, tightness, or restlessness.
3
Expose the bargain“My mind is promising relief now while hiding the later cost.”
4
Change your physical stateStand up, walk, breathe slowly, drink water, or step outside.
5
DelayYou are not saying “never.” You are refusing automatic action.
6
Choose from values“What action would make me trust myself more tomorrow?”
